India's Multi-Billion Dollar Skin Lightening Industry



For decades in South Asian cultures, the idea that whiter skin tones are more attractive has created an insane obsession with skin lightening, ranging from bleach creams, medical procedures, and at home remedies. The leading brand, Fair and Lovely, has become a household favorite. The brand uses ads featuring several famous celebrities to promote the product and show that with this cream, you can be as beautiful as them.
